Who We Are

Bruno's Pizzeria was bought to Australia in the early 80s, by an inspired young man named Bruno. Having scored the perfect location, within the heart of Hawthron. Bruno set out to bring his Italian heritage to everyone's bellies.

The reason you're probably looking at our website right now is because you're looking for dinner. We can guarantee you'll have the biggest smile and happiest belly after taking your first bite of our delicious pizzas.

If you're looking to dine-in, we can provide professional restaurant quality service. Or if you're looking to binge your favourite TV show on the couch, we can deliver right to your door
